# Travel Rules & Heuristics

## Bengaluru
- **Mode: Metro**
  - Crowd: High (Peak), Low (Off-peak)
  - Time Factor: 1.0 (Consistent)
  - Health Karma: +5 (Walking to station)
  - Carbon Score: +10
- **Mode: Auto**
  - Crowd: Solo
  - Time Factor: 1.5 (Traffic dependent)
  - Health Karma: -2 (Stress)
  - Carbon Score: -5

## Mumbai
- **Mode: Local Train**
  - Crowd: Extreme
  - Time Factor: 0.8 (Fastest)
  - Health Karma: +10 (Superhuman agility required)
  - Carbon Score: +10
- **Mode: BEST Bus**
  - Crowd: High
  - Time Factor: 2.0 (Traffic)
  - Health Karma: +2
  - Carbon Score: +5

## Delhi
