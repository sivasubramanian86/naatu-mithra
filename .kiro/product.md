# NaatuMithra: Multi-City Product Definition

NaatuMithra is a contextual companion designed to help users navigate the nuanced landscape of Indian cities. It focuses on:
- **Linguistic Nuance**: Translating local slang into understandable English/emojis.
- **Gastronomic Moods**: Mapping how you feel to what you should eat.
- **Micro-Mobility**: Predicting the best way to travel based on hyper-local data.
- **Cultural Heritage**: Providing bite-sized heritage facts that are shareable.

## Local Nuance Breakdown

### Bengaluru (blr)
- **Slang**: "Solpa Adjust Maadi" (Adjust slightly), "Maga" (Friend/Bro), "Ondu Half" (One half coffee).
- **Food**: Vidyarthi Bhavan Dosas, Filter Coffee, MTR Meals.
- **Transit**: Namma Metro, Silk Board Traffic jokes, Yulu Bikes.

### Mumbai (bom)
- **Slang**: "Bantai" (Brother), "Khali Peeli" (Unnecessarily), "Shaana" (Smart/Arrogant).
- **Food**: Vada Pav, Misal Pav, Cutting Chai at Marine Drive.
- **Transit**: Local Trains (Lifeline), Kaali-Peeli Taxis, BEST Buses.

### Delhi (del)
- **Slang**: "Bhai" (The universal prefix), "Jugaad" (Resourceful fix), "Velli" (Idle).
- **Food**: Old Delhi Chole Bhature, Chandni Chowk Paranthas, Butter Chicken.
- **Transit**: Delhi Metro, E-Rickshaws, Hired Cabs.

### Chennai (maa)
- **Slang**: "Machan" (Friend), "Enna Kodumai Saravanan" (Classic movie reference for frustration), "Vaada" (Come here).
- **Food**: Marina Beach Sundal, Filter Kaapi, Idli-Sambar from Saravana Bhavan.
- **Transit**: Chennai Metro, Suburban Trains, Shared Autos.

### Kolkata (ccu)
- **Slang**: "Dada/Didi" (Elder brother/sister), "Bhalo" (Good), "Kyabla" (Innocent fool).
- **Food**: Puchka, Sandesh, Kolkata Biryani (with the potato).
- **Transit**: Yellow Taxis, Kolkata Tram (heritage), Tuktuk.

### Hyderabad (hyd)
- **Slang**: "Hau" (Yes), "Nakko" (No), "Baigan ke baatan" (Silly talk).
- **Food**: Paradise Biryani, Haleem (during Ramzan), Irani Chai at Nimrah.
- **Transit**: TSRTC Buses, Hyderabad Metro, Autos.

### Pune (pnq)
- **Slang**: "Puneri Patya" (Sarcastic signboards), "Layi Bhaari" (Too good), "Yedya" (Crazy).
- **Food**: Misal Pav at Bedekar, Mastani, Puran Poli.
- **Transit**: PMPML Buses, Pune Metro, Two-wheelers everywhere.

### Ahmedabad (amd)
- **Slang**: "Su che?" (What's up?), "Majama" (Fine), "Locha" (Mess/Food name).
- **Food**: Dhokla, Fafda-Jalebi, Khaman.
- **Transit**: AMTS, BRTS, Auto-rickshaws.

### Jaipur (jai)
- **Slang**: "Padharo Mhare Desh" (Welcome to my land), "Khamma Ghani" (Royal greeting).
- **Food**: Dal Baati Churma, Pyaaz Kachori, Ghevar.
- **Transit**: Cycle Rickshaws in Pink City, Low-floor Buses.

### Kochi (cok)
- **Slang**: "Aliya" (Friend/Brother), "Sakhave" (Comrade), "Poli" (Awesome).
- **Food**: Appam with Stew, Puttu Kadala, Karimeen Pollichathu.
- **Transit**: Kochi Water Metro, Private Buses (speedy).

### Lucknow (lko)
- **Slang**: "Ama yaar" (Casual exasperation), "Pehle Aap" (The culture of courtesy).
- **Food**: Tunday Kababs, Galouti Kabab, Basket Chaat.
- **Transit**: Lucknow Metro, E-Rickshaws.

### Varanasi (vns)
- **Slang**: "Guru" (Respectful address), "Bhang" (Sacred herb), "Ghat" (The heart of the city).
- **Food**: Banarasi Paan, Kachori-Sabzi, Rose Lassi.
- **Transit**: Walking the Gallis, Ghat Boats.

### Chandigarh (ixc)
- **Slang**: "Gedi Route" (Driving circuit), "Vailly" (Free person), "Ghaint" (Cool).
- **Food**: Amritsari Kulcha (nearby), Chole Bhature, Lassi.
- **Transit**: CTU Buses, Cycle-sharing.

### Amritsar (atq)
- **Slang**: "Balle Balle" (Expression of joy), "Chakk De" (Let's do it).
- **Food**: Golden Temple Langar, Amritsari Kulcha, Kesar Da Dhaba.
- **Transit**: E-Rickshaws, Walking the Heritage Street.

### Indore (ind)
- **Slang**: "Bhiya" (Brother), "Hao" (Yes), "Gajab" (Strange/Amazing).
- **Food**: Poha-Jalebi, Sarafa Night Market delicacies.
- **Transit**: iBus (BRTS), Chalo app users.

### Tirunelveli (tny)
- **Slang**: "Yela" (Universal call/Hey), "Enga irukka?" (Where are you?), "Sothukkullada" (Inside the meal).
- **Food**: Tirunelveli Halwa (Iruttu Kadai), Vellai Appam, Kootanchoru.
- **Transit**: Town Buses, Shared Autos, Thamirabarani Riverside walks.

### Coimbatore (cjb)
- **Slang**: "Namma Ooru" (Our city), "Enga ponga?" (Where are you going? - with respect), "Kongu" (Regional dialect).
- **Food**: Annapoorna Sambar, Arisiyum Paruppum, Kaalaan.
- **Transit**: Private Buses (fastest in the world), Coimbatore Metro (planned), Two-wheelers.

### Trichy (trz)
- **Slang**: "Rockfort" (The landmark), "Anna" (Elder brother), "Poitu varen" (I'll go and come back).
- **Food**: Banana chips from Rockfort, Srirangam Puliyodharai, Jigar Thanda.
- **Transit**: Town Buses, Autos to Srirangam, Cycle Rickshaws.

### Madurai (ixm)
- **Slang**: "Annachi" (Respectful address), "Maduraikaaran" (A person from Madurai), "Athaan" (Uncle/Husband - regional).
- **Food**: Jigar Thanda, Bun Parotta, Madurai Mutton Biryani.
- **Transit**: 24/7 Town Buses (Thoonga Nagaram), Shared Autos, Walking the Four Masi Streets.

### Trivandrum (trv)
- **Slang**: "Appi" (Brother/Friend - local informal), "Santhosham" (Happiness), "Poli" (Great).
- **Food**: Pazham Pori & Beef, Trivandrum Chicken Fry, Boli and Palpayasam.
- **Transit**: KSRTC Buses (Aana Vandi), Trivandrum Metro (planned), Autorickshaws.
